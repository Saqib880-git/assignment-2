P.E.T. Trust (R)

People’s education society was founded by Nithya Sachiva Sri. K.V. Shankara gowda. His vision was to provide to the people of Mandya a prestigious educational institution-which would focus on quality education. Students of Mandya district in particular and students of neighbouring districts, in general, have been benefited from the institution. The demise of Sri K.V.Shankara gowda made way to Sahakara Rathna Sri Dr.H.D Chowdaiah as a chairman of the Society After his retirement the mantle of chairmanship has been passed on to the young and energetic Sri K.S.Vijay Anand. People’s Education Society(P.E.S.) is now called People’s Education Trust(P.E.T).

About Our College

Welcome to Our Institution

P.E.S. College of Science, Arts & Commerce Established in 1965

The Institution P.E.S. College of Science, Arts & Commerce, is a dream child of late Sri K.V.Shankara Gowda, former Education Minister – Government of Karnataka, an educationist, politician, artist, entrepreneur and a social worker. Later Dr. H.D.Chowdaiah, former Member of Legislative Assembly and council, a well known educationist and a pioneer in the field of Co operation, was the Chairman of the Trust. Sri H. Honnappa, Former Member of Legislative council, was the General Secretary, Sri S. Panchalingu, Joint Secretary and Vice Presidents Sriyuths Basavaiah & G.K.Ramegowda were the office bearers of the Trust. At Present,

  • Sri K.S.Vijay Anand, grandson of late Sri K.V.Shankara Gowda,
  • son of late Sri K.S.Sachidananda,
  • former ZP member,
  • Young and energetic Youth Leader, is the Chairman of the Trust.
  • Sri K.R.Dayananda, Retired Engineer from Government of Karnataka, is Joint Secretary.
